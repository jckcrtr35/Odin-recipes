Corndog

Description

Breaded meat on a stick. Need I say more?

Ingredients

  • Oven
  • Mustard
  • Box of Corndogs from Kroger
  • Plate

Steps

  1. Set all Ingredients onto preparation station
  2. Remove x corndogs from the Box
  3. Heat in Oven for 20 minutes on 400F
  4. Remove from oven when it dings, and lather corndogs in mustard
