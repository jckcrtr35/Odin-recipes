Grilled Cheese

Description

The almighty king of kings. The legenDAIRY, always satisfying, Grilled Cheese.

Ingredients

  • Butter Knife
  • Butter
  • 2 Slices of bread
  • 4 different Cheeses of your choosing
  • Burner and Pan

Steps

  1. Lather each side of both slices of bread with butter.
  2. Turn on burner to medium heat and place frying pan on burner.
  3. Place 4 different cheeses betwix the slices of bread.
  4. Place sandwich onto frying pan, and flip after 3 minutes.
  5. Cook bread until golden brown on each side, and be sure the cheese has melted the bread together.
  6. (Optional) Cut sandwich in a triangular shape.
  7. Enjoy the simplest and greatest sandwich in the world!
