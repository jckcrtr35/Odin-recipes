Peanut Butter and Jelly

Description

Peanut Butter and Jelly sandwiches are a classic snack for anyone. They are easily crafted for those on the go.

Ingredients

  • Butter Knife
  • Peanut Butter
  • 2 Slices of bread
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Set all Ingredients onto preparation station
  2. Using the Butter Knife, gather Peanut Butter and lather it onto the bread. Use both slices of bread to spread if necessary.
  3. Repeat process with the jelly, but keep in mind that you may not need as much jelly as peanut butter.
  4. Press lathered slices of bread together, and leave knife over the sink in case you want to make another for later.
